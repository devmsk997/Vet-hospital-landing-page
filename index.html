Our Services

Complete Veterinary Care Under One Roof

We offer a full spectrum of veterinary services to ensure your pet stays healthy, happy, and thriving at every stage of life.

Wellness Exams

Comprehensive health assessments to keep your pet in peak condition with personalized care plans.

Vaccinations

Core and lifestyle vaccinations tailored to your pet's needs, age, and risk factors.

Surgery

From routine spays and neuters to advanced soft-tissue procedures, performed with precision and care.

Emergency Care

Urgent veterinary care when your pet needs it most, with 24/7 availability for critical cases.

Diagnostics

In-house lab, digital X-rays, and ultrasound for fast and accurate diagnoses.

Pharmacy

On-site pharmacy with prescription medications and preventative treatments for convenience.

Dental Care

Professional cleanings, extractions, and oral health assessments to keep teeth healthy.

Microchipping

Permanent pet identification to ensure your companion can always find their way home.

About Us

Caring for Pets Like Family Since 2010

Westside Veterinary Clinic was founded with a simple mission: to provide compassionate, high-quality veterinary care in a warm and welcoming environment. Our team of dedicated professionals treats every patient with the love and attention they deserve.

We believe that preventive care, open communication, and a fear-free experience are the foundations of excellent veterinary medicine. Our modern facility is equipped with the latest technology to deliver the best outcomes for your pets.
